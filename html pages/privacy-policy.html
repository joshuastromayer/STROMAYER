Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 22/11/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to CodeItClear. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

We collect the following personal information:

  • Name (via forms)
  • Email address (via forms)

b. Information Automatically Collected

We do not collect IP addresses, browser details, or location data directly. However, Google AdSense may collect such information for targeted advertisements.

c. Use of Local Storage

We use localStorage to manage subscriptions and improve the user experience, such as tracking whether a user is subscribed.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Send newsletters and updates.
  • Improve the website and content offerings.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not share personal data with third parties. Only aggregated, anonymized data (e.g., page views) is shared for analytics and advertising purposes.

5. Advertising

We use Google AdSense for targeted advertisements, which may collect and process certain data as per their policies. For more information, please refer to Google's Privacy & Terms.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take all reasonable measures to ensure the protection of your information.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as you are subscribed. If you unsubscribe, we will securely delete your data within 6 months.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request to unsubscribe and stop receiving newsletters.
  • Request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

9. Contact Information

If you have any concerns about privacy or data usage, please reach out to us at:

Email: legal@stromayer.com

10. Location of Services

Stromayer is based in the United Kingdom, and the website is hosted on GitHub Pages.

11. Children's Privacy

The website is suitable for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

12. Policy Updates

If our privacy policy changes, we will notify subscribed users via email.
